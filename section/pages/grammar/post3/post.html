Welcome to Level 3!

In this level, we will explore more complex English grammar. You will work on questions about future tense, conjunctions, comparative adjectives, reflexive pronouns, modal verbs, and prepositional phrases. Challenge yourself and show your English skills!

Little English - Level 2

1. Choose the correct future tense form:

"Tomorrow, she ___ (go) to the market."

2. Choose the correct conjunction:

"I wanted to go for a walk, ___ it was raining."

3. Choose the correct comparative adjective:

"She is ___ than her brother."

4. Choose the correct reflexive pronoun:

"He looked at ___ in the mirror."

5. Choose the correct modal verb:

"You ___ finish your homework before you go out."

6. Choose the correct prepositional phrase:

"The cat is hiding ___ the bed."
