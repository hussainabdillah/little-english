Welcome to Level 2!

In this level, we will deepen our understanding of English grammar. You will face questions on possessive pronouns, past tense, adverbs, and prepositions. Let's continue your learning journey and improve your language skills!

Little English - Level 2

1. Choose the correct possessive pronoun:

"This is ___ book."

2. Choose the correct form of the verb:

"Yesterday, they ___ to the zoo."

3. Choose the correct adverb:

"She ran ___."

4. Choose the correct preposition:

"The book is ___ the table."

5. Choose the correct verb (to be):

"They ___ very happy."

6. Choose the correct possessive pronoun:

"That toy is ___."
