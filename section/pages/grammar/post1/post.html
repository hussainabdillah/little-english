Welcome to Level 1!

In this level, we will learn the basics of English grammar. You will answer questions about nouns, pronouns, verbs, adjectives and articles. Let's get started and good luck!

1. Choose the correct noun:

"There are many ___ in the park."

2. Choose the correct pronoun:

"___ is my best friend."

3. Choose the correct verb:

"She ___ every day."

4. Choose the correct adjective:

"The cat is ___."

5. Choose the correct article:

"I saw ___ elephant at the zoo."

6. Choose the correct noun:

"The ___ is flying in the sky."
